Survey Data:

Solon designed a survey for The Video Game History Foundation to run from May 7th, 2026 to June 3rd. It was posted at the top of the VGHF website for any browsers to click on and a call-to-action was added to the monthly memo in their email listserv. This led to 144 responses which was pared down to 135 considered valuable data. The survey was designed to find out who the VGHF's natural users are

These responses were separated into seven user roles: Indie Historians, Pro Historians, Indie Devs, Pro Devs, Educators/Students, Library/Archive/Museum workers, and Enthusiasts/Collectors. 

This folder has four sets of data:
- (2) Spreadsheets of raw and processed results (.xlsx)
- (8) Documents with results processed into graphs (.pdf)
- (1) Written version of all questions asked to users, includes survey logic (.docx)
- (1) Image used as background for survey (.jpg)
