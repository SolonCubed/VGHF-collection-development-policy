# VGHF Collection Development Policy - 2026 UW Capstone (MLIS)
A Collection Development Policy and the research that went into its development for the Video Game History Foundation. Includes all associated data from the UW iSchool Capstone Project (2025-2026)

Table of Contents:
- Collection Development Policy - The final CDP to be developed by the VGHF and associated deliverables
- Errata - Associated data and materials used to better analyze VGHF
- Interview Data - Interviews with Games Archivists and Librarians to establish understandings of videogame heritage and how they apply to policy development
- iSchool Capstone Files - Presentation materials to develop project as designed by the University of Washington's iSchool for the 2025-2026 school year
- Survey Data - All raw and processed data used in constructing The Video Game History Foundation Preservation Survey

Thank you for your participation in video game librarianship! This study was approved by the University of Washington IRB #STUDY00024808.
